Traditional Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice, often accompanied by raw fish, vegetables, and seaweed. It is a healthy and delicious option for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sushi rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Fresh fish (like salmon or tuna)
  • Cucumber, avocado, and carrots (for vegetables)
  • Soy sauce for serving

Method

  1. Rinse the sushi rice until the water runs clear. Cook the rice with water in a rice cooker or pot until tender.
  2. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl. Pour over the cooked rice and stir gently to season it. Let it cool.
  3. Prepare the fillings: Slice the fish, cucumber, avocado, and carrots into thin strips.
  4. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat. Spread a thin layer of seasoned rice on the nori, leaving a border.
  5. Add the fillings in a line across the rice. Roll the sushi tightly using the mat.
  6. Slice the roll into pieces and serve with soy sauce.
